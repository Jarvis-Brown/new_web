Overview

There are a lot of apps available for people to enjoy music. Your Tunes is an app for people to easily customize their listening experience based on what's currently popular and their musical preferences. Your Tunes focuses on ages 21 to 45 as these are the ages that are most active during the day and need something that keeps them going!

Role

UI/UX Designer

Challenge

I created Your Tunes because I spend most of my day listening to music myself to get through the work day like most people. Music apps like Spotify and Apple are complex with a variety of things to explore and often change their UI which can confuse the user.

Competition

Apple Music

  • Has too many options other than music like movies, tv shows, etc. that users can get lost in. Your Tunes wants users to focus on whatever task they have at hand.

Spotify

  • Often changes its UI without notifying its users. Your Tunes keeps everything the same. Simple and easy to use so users are familiar with its UI and able to easily get around the app.

Youtube Music

  • Unable to share singular songs with others. Your Tunes allows its users to share any song of their choice with their friends and family.

Solution

I created an app that only supports music. Your Tunes focuses on simplicity so the user can easily customize their musical listening experience that doesn't distract them from completing their tasks. 

Key Feature

Your Tunes focuses on keeping things short and simple for its users.

  • Create playlists and libraries
  • Quickly browse genres
  • Artists and songs that are listened to often are saved on the home page for easy access
  • Share music with others
